Get Codox

Codox turns exam PDFs into ready-to-import Triviadox question sets. It runs on your own device, with your own free Gemini key.

Fastest: use it in your browser

Nothing to install — Codox is a web app that works offline once opened.

Open Codox

On a school-managed Windows laptop that blocks installing programs, use this browser version — it is the same app.

Windows

Download Codox_…_x64-setup.exe from the latest release and run it. After that, Codox updates itself — no re-downloading.

Download for Windows
  1. Windows may show a blue “Windows protected your PC” screen. That appears for any new app that isn't from a big company.
  2. Click More info, then Run anyway.

Android

Download codox-….apk from the latest release and open it.

Download for Android
  1. Your phone will ask to “allow installs from this source” — allow it once and tap Install.

iPhone & iPad — two steps

Open Codox in Safari, then:

  1. Tap the Share button (the square with the arrow, at the bottom of Safari).
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add. Codox appears like a normal app.
